## About Me

I'm a developer who likes building things that are actually fun to use.

Most of my projects end up being some mix of desktop apps, utilities, experiments, and tools I wanted badly enough to go make myself. I spend a lot of time working with Python, C#, JavaScript, and UI-heavy projects, and I care a lot about making stuff feel polished instead of just functional.

I like learning by building, breaking things, fixing them, reworking the UI ten times, and slowly turning a rough idea into something real.
